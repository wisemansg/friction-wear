## ğŸ§® Holm-Archard Wear Model

Wear volume \( V \) calculated:

V = K * (F_N / H) * d


Where:  
- \( K \) = wear coefficient  
- \( F_N \) = normal load  
- \( H \) = hardness  
- \( d \) = sliding distance  

Synthetic oil produced lower calculated wear volumes than mineral oil at both 100 kg & 200 kg loads.
